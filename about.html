About Rahman News Desk

Independent on-scene reporting & photography across New York City.

Rahman News Desk is an independent New York City–based news and media outlet focused on real-time incident reporting, emergency response coverage, and on-scene photography.

Coverage includes breaking incidents involving FDNY, NYPD, EMS, vehicle fires, structural fires, accidents, police activity, and other public-safety events across NYC.

All content is captured and reported directly from the scene, prioritizing accuracy, clarity, and public awareness.
