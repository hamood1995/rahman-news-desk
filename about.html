About Rahman News Desk

Rahman News Desk is an independent on-scene reporting and digital news platform focused on breaking incidents and public safety coverage across New York City.

We document and publish real-time information related to fires, police activity, emergency responses, traffic incidents, and significant public events. Coverage is gathered directly from the scene whenever possible, supported by observation, verified visual documentation, and publicly available information.

Rahman News Desk operates independently and is not affiliated with any government agency, law enforcement department, or emergency service organization.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide timely, accurate, and responsible reporting from the streets of New York City — giving the public clear visibility into incidents as they unfold, while maintaining respect for emergency responders, affected individuals, and ongoing investigations.

What We Cover

  • Fire incidents and emergency responses
  • Police activity and public safety operations
  • Traffic disruptions and vehicle incidents
  • Major neighborhood events and developing situations
  • On-scene photography and visual documentation

Editorial Standards

  • Information collected through direct observation and available sources
  • Details may evolve as official statements are released
  • No speculation presented as fact
  • Sensitive situations covered responsibly
