FDNY Responds to Dollar Van Fire on Flatbush Avenue

Published: December 2, 2025
Time observed: Approximately 10:20 AM
Borough: Brooklyn
Location: Flatbush Avenue & Avenue D

A dollar van caught fire on the morning of December 2, 2025, at the intersection of Flatbush Avenue and Avenue D in Brooklyn.

Firefighters from the FDNY responded to the scene within minutes of the incident and were able to quickly bring the fire under control. The affected vehicle was secured, and the fire was prevented from spreading.

No injuries were reported as a result of the incident. Emergency personnel remained on scene briefly to ensure the area was safe before clearing.

This incident was covered on scene.

On-scene notes

  • Agency response: FDNY
  • Incident type: Vehicle fire (dollar van)
  • Traffic impact: Temporary disruption near intersection
  • Injuries: None reported
